Cookie Policy

Effective Date: 30-08-2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Jyoti Software Pvt. Ltd uses cookies and similar technologies on our websites and services. By using our site, you agree to the use of cookies as described here.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store data that can be retrieved by a web server in the domain that placed the cookie. We also use similar technologies like pixels and local storage.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary: Required for basic site functionality and security.
  • Performance/Analytics: Help us understand site usage to improve performance.
  • Functional: Remember preferences such as language and region.
  • Advertising: Used to deliver relevant ads (where applicable).

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third parties (e.g., analytics providers, embedded content, payment gateways). Their use is subject to their own privacy policies.

Managing Cookies

  • Control cookies via your browser settings (block, delete, or be notified).
  • Blocking some cookies may impact site functionality.

Do Not Track
