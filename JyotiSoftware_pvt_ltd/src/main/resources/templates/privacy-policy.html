At Jyoti Software Pvt. Ltd, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our websites, products, and services.

What Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

a. Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.
  • Payment information for purchases and subscriptions.
  • Account login details (usernames, passwords).

b. Technical Information

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, time on page, interactions.

c. Other Information

  • Feedback, inquiries, or customer support requests.
  • Data from third-party integrations or partners (if applicable).

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve our products and services.
  • To personalize content, recommendations, and communications.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • To communicate with you (including service updates and marketing).
  • To analyze usage and improve performance and security.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, hosting providers, analytics, and support tools.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

Your Choices

You can opt out of marketing emails using the unsubscribe link or by contacting us. You may also request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data (subject to law).

Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we implement appropriate safeguards.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post changes on this page and update the effective date.

Contact Us

For questions or requests regarding this policy, contact: info@jyotisoftware.in
